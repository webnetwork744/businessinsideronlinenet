Commercial vs Residential Real Estate

Understanding the difference between commercial and residential properties is key for investors and sellers:

  • Commercial Properties: Shops, offices, warehouses, and industrial plots. Typically provide rental income and business opportunities.
  • Residential Properties: Flats, villas, apartments, and residential plots. Ideal for long-term investment and family living.
  • Both can be sold locally or to overseas buyers depending on market demand and legal permissions.
