Setup Your Product Delivery Business Online & Get Angel Investments

Move your delivery business online, integrate third-party shipping, and attract investors to scale your startup.

Step 1: Digitize Your Delivery Business

  • Create a professional website with product listings and delivery options.
  • Set up an online portal for orders, tracking, and customer support.
  • Integrate payment gateways for secure online transactions.
  • Ensure mobile-friendly design for better accessibility.

Step 2: Integrate Third-Party Shipping Services

  • Partner with reliable logistics and courier companies.
  • Enable automated shipping label generation and tracking.
  • Offer multiple delivery options to customers.
  • Monitor shipping performance and customer satisfaction regularly.

Step 3: Prepare for Angel Investment

  • Create a comprehensive business plan highlighting growth potential.
  • Showcase your online platform, order management, and delivery efficiency.
  • Prepare financial projections and investor pitch decks.
  • Highlight scalability and market opportunities in the delivery sector.

Step 4: Identify and Pitch to Angel Investors

  • Research angel investor networks focused on logistics and e-commerce startups.
  • Attend investor meetups, startup expos, and online funding platforms.
  • Tailor your pitch to show ROI, growth strategy, and competitive advantage.
  • Negotiate investment terms and maintain transparency to secure funding.
