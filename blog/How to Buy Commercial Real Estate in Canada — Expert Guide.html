Q: Can foreigners buy commercial real estate in Canada?
A: Yes, but foreign investors must follow Canadian regulations and may require additional approvals.
Q: What types of commercial properties are best for beginners?
A: Multi-unit residential, retail, or small office properties are commonly recommended for first-time investors.
Q: How much down payment is required?
A: Typically 20-35% of the purchase price depending on property type, financing method, and investor profile.
