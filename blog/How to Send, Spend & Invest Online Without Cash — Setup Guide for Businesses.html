Detailed setup: Tools & actions

  • Digital wallets & P2P: Sign up and verify accounts with PayPal Business, Wise, Revolut Business, Skrill or regional equivalents. Use wallets for quick transfers and holding balances.
  • Payment gateways: Integrate Stripe, PayPal Checkout, or local gateways to accept cards, bank redirects and e‑wallets on your website.
  • Virtual bank accounts & IBANs: Open virtual accounts to receive local currency transfers without a physical bank branch.
  • Virtual cards & expense controls: Use virtual/virtual‑plastic cards for online spending, with per-card limits and auditing.
  • Invoicing & reconciliation: Use invoicing tools built into your gateway or accounting platforms to match incoming funds automatically.
  • Investment platforms: Choose regulated brokers or robo‑advisors that accept bank/wallet funding to invest excess working capital.

Frequently asked questions

Can I run an online business with no cash at all?

Yes. With digital wallets, payment gateways, virtual cards and bank transfers you can accept, hold and disburse funds without physical cash. Ensure you follow local regulations and tax rules.

How do I move money internationally cheaply?

Use specialist providers (e.g., Wise) and virtual IBANs to reduce conversion fees and get better FX rates than traditional banks.

Is investing business funds online safe?

Use regulated brokers, diversify investments, and keep business operational cash separate from investment accounts. Consult a financial advisor for tailored advice.
