Add Business or Real Estate Partners in Bitrix24 CRM

Step-by-Step Guide to Manage Partners and Collaborators Remotely

Introduction

Bitrix24 CRM allows you to manage business partners, real estate collaborators, and client relationships remotely. Adding partners ensures better deal tracking, automated notifications, and seamless collaboration across global teams.

Step 1: Set Up Your Bitrix24 Account

  • Sign up or log in at Bitrix24.com.
  • Complete your company profile with name, logo, timezone, and currency.
  • Invite team members who will manage partners.
  • Ensure admin privileges for partner management.

Step 2: Navigate to the CRM Section

  • Click CRM in the dashboard sidebar.
  • Select Contacts or Companies to categorize partners.
  • Decide partner type: Individual Contact or Company.

Step 3: Adding a New Partner

  • Click Add Contact or Add Company.
  • Fill in details: Name, Email, Phone, Location, and Type of Partnership.
  • Assign a responsible manager from your team for follow-ups.
  • Use custom fields or tags like "Real Estate Partner" for easy filtering.

Step 4: Assigning Deals or Opportunities

  • Navigate to CRM → Deals and click Add Deal.
  • Select the partner in the Responsible or Associated field.
  • Track interactions, negotiations, and commissions linked to the partner.
  • Set automated notifications for deal progress.

Step 5: Collaboration and Communication

  • Use Bitrix24 Chat or Feed to communicate with partners remotely.
  • Share documents via Drive or attach files to contacts/deals.
  • Schedule tasks and reminders using Tasks & Projects.
  • Use video calls within Bitrix24 for remote meetings.

Step 6: Setting Permissions

  • Go to Settings → CRM Permissions.
  • Define access levels: full access, limited access, or view-only.
  • Protect sensitive information while enabling collaboration.

Step 7: Tracking Performance

  • Use CRM Reports to monitor partner activity: Deals closed, Revenue generated, Tasks completed.
  • Set KPIs and schedule automated reporting.
  • Use dashboards for real-time partner performance overview.

Step 8: Using Automation for Remote Partner Management

  • Configure automation rules for partner workflows.
  • Auto-assign new leads, send notifications, and update partner status.
  • Reduce manual work and ensure timely communication.
