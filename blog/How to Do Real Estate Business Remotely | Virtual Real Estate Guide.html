Why Real Estate Remotely?

Thanks to technology, real estate business can now be conducted from anywhere. From virtual tours and remote property closings to digital marketing and online property management, remote real estate offers flexibility, global reach, and efficiency.

Steps to Do Real Estate Business Remotely

  • Build a professional website showcasing your real estate services.
  • Leverage virtual tours, 3D walkthroughs, and video calls for property showings.
  • Use online platforms like Zillow, Realtor.com, and MLS for listing properties.
  • Adopt e-signature and digital contract tools for remote closings.
  • Utilize social media ads to reach buyers and investors worldwide.
  • Partner with local property managers for on-ground operations.

Best Tools for Remote Real Estate

Here are the most effective tools for running a remote real estate business:

  • CRM Platforms: Manage leads and client communication efficiently.
  • Virtual Tour Software: Showcase properties with Matterport or EyeSpy360.
  • Contract Tools: DocuSign, HelloSign for secure e-signatures.
  • Property Management Apps: Buildium, AppFolio for remote landlord tasks.
  • Video Platforms: Zoom or Google Meet for consultations and virtual tours.
