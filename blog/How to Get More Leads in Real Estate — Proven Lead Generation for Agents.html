FAQ — quick answers

Q: What's the fastest way to get more leads?
A: Run a focused local ad for a single listing or upcoming open house and follow up within 24 hours with a clear CTA.
Q: Can I generate leads without ads?
A: Yes — optimize GMB, publish neighborhood content, ask for referrals, and host value-packed local events.
Q: How should I prioritize channels?
A: Test 2 channels at once (organic + paid). Measure CPA and lead quality for 30 days, then scale the winner.
