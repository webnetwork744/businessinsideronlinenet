FAQ

Can I really start without paying anything?

Yes — many methods require time and effort instead of money. Avoid sites that charge fees to apply.

Which method pays fastest?

Microtasks, user testing, and short freelancing gigs usually pay fastest; content and affiliate income grow over time.

How do I prove payment to clients?

Use clear invoices, redacted screenshots of transactions, and testimonials to build credibility.
