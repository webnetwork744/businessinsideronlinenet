Step‑by‑Step Roadmap

1

Find a Market & Validate

Identify a niche with clear pain points. Validate with quick experiments (surveys, landing pages, pre‑orders) before building.

2

Create a Minimum Viable Offer

Launch a simple product: a digital course, consulting package, freelance service, or pre‑sale of a product. Focus on delivering value fast.

3

Get Your First 10 Customers

Use outreach, partnerships, content, and ads. Early customers are feedback machines — iterate on price and delivery.

4

Automate Sales & Delivery

Set up funnels, email sequences, and payment automation. Use scheduling tools and templates to reduce manual work.

5

Scale Predictably

Invest in paid acquisition, affiliate partners, or a productized service model. Outsource tasks that don't require your expertise.

6

Plan the Exit from Your Job

Set financial milestones (e.g., 80–100% of net salary for 3 months) before quitting. Build an emergency fund and a transition checklist.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need savings before quitting?

Yes — it's wise to have an emergency fund (3–6 months) and enough runway for business investments. Aim for predictable revenue before resigning.

Can I start while working full time?

Absolutely — many founders validate and launch on evenings/weekends. Use strict time blocks and prioritize highest‑impact tasks.

How do I avoid burnout?

Time‑block, take regular micro‑breaks, delegate, and maintain social and physical activities to protect wellbeing.
