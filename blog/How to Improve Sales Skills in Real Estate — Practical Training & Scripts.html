Practical methods to improve sales skills

These are short, focused practices that top agents use to continuously improve their conversion skills.

1. Script + Roleplay

Write short scripts for the 3 most-common objections and roleplay them weekly with a partner for 15 minutes.

2. Time-to-Contact Routine

Respond to inbound leads within 10 minutes. Use templated first messages that open for conversation (not just information).

3. Listing Presentation Drill

Practice your listing presentation as a 10-minute pitch; record and refine tone, pacing, and value statements.

FAQ — quick answers

Q: How long until I see improvement?
A: With 15 minutes of focused practice daily (script + roleplay), most agents notice measurable improvement in 2–4 weeks.
Q: Do I need a coach?
A: A coach accelerates progress, but consistent peer roleplay and structured drills can also produce strong results.
Q: Can this work for teams?
A: Yes — teams benefit from shared scripts, group roleplay sessions, and a single CRM cadence to track outcomes.
