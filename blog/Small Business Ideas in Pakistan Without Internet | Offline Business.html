Small Business Ideas in Pakistan Without Internet

Explore offline business opportunities to earn money locally and grow your income.

1. Grocery or Convenience Store

Starting a small grocery or convenience store in your neighborhood is a reliable offline business. It requires low investment and steady demand.

2. Tailoring and Stitching Services

If you have sewing skills, offer tailoring services to your local community. This is a traditional offline business that can bring consistent income.

3. Home-Based Bakery or Catering

Prepare homemade cakes, snacks, or meals and sell them locally. Home-based food businesses are very popular in Pakistan and can grow with word-of-mouth marketing.

4. Street Food or Mobile Food Stalls

  • Chaat, pakoras, or tea stalls
  • Fresh juice or cold drink carts
  • Snack delivery in busy markets

5. Handicrafts and Local Products

Create and sell handmade crafts, pottery, or decorative items in local markets. This offline business is ideal for artistic individuals looking to earn extra income.

Other Offline Business Ideas in Pakistan

  • Bookstore or stationery shop
  • Car or bike rental services
  • Event management and local party services
  • Plant nursery or gardening business
