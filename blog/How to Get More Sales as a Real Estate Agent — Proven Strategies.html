FAQ — quick answers

Q: What's the fastest way to get more sales as an agent?
A: Run a focused local ad for one listing, host a well-promoted open house, and follow up the same day with every lead.
Q: Can I get leads without paid ads?
A: Yes — optimize Google Business Profile, publish neighborhood content, ask for referrals, and use organic social with targeted local posts.
Q: How do I measure what works?
A: Track lead source, response time, and conversion rate in your CRM; A/B test ad creatives and landing pages weekly.
