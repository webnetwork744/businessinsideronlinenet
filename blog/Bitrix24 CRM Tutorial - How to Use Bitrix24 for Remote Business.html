Bitrix24 CRM Tutorial

Step-by-Step Guide to Use Bitrix24 for Leads, Deals, Projects, and Remote Team Management

Introduction to Bitrix24 CRM

Bitrix24 CRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management software that helps businesses manage leads, sales, clients, tasks, and projects remotely. Ideal for small, medium, and global businesses, it offers automation, analytics, and communication tools all in one platform.

  • Manage leads and contacts efficiently.
  • Track deals through a visual sales pipeline.
  • Collaborate with remote teams using tasks and projects.
  • Automate sales and communication workflows.
  • Generate real-time reports and analytics for informed decisions.

Step 1: Setting Up Your Bitrix24 Account

  • Go to Bitrix24.com and sign up for free.
  • Verify your email and log in to your dashboard.
  • Configure your company profile: name, logo, timezone, and currency.
  • Set user roles for your team and invite members.

Tip: Use a real company email for better client communication and CRM notifications.

Step 2: Configuring CRM Settings

  • Navigate to CRM > Settings.
  • Customize lead and deal stages according to your sales process.
  • Add custom fields for your industry-specific data.
  • Set notifications for new leads, deals, or client messages.
  • Integrate your email to automatically capture incoming leads.

Step 3: Adding Leads and Contacts

  • Go to CRM > Leads and click Add Lead.
  • Enter client information: name, email, phone, company, and lead source.
  • Assign leads to team members for follow-up.
  • Import multiple leads using CSV or Excel files.

Step 4: Managing Deals

  • Navigate to CRM > Deals and create a new deal.
  • Link the deal to a lead or contact.
  • Track deals through stages: New → Contacted → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed.
  • Attach files, log emails, and update status regularly.
  • Use automation rules to move deals automatically when criteria are met.

Step 5: Email Marketing and Communication

  • Go to CRM > Marketing > Email Campaigns.
  • Create campaigns targeting leads or contacts.
  • Use pre-built templates or design custom HTML emails.
  • Schedule campaigns or send immediately and monitor open/click rates.
  • Segment audiences for better engagement and higher conversion rates.

Step 6: Tasks and Project Management

  • Navigate to Tasks & Projects in the sidebar.
  • Create tasks, assign team members, set deadlines and priorities.
  • Link tasks to deals or clients for context.
  • Track progress using Kanban boards, Gantt charts, or task lists.

Step 7: Reporting and Analytics

  • Go to CRM > Reports for insights on sales and performance.
  • Generate reports on leads, deals, revenue, and team activities.
  • Export reports to Excel or schedule automatic recurring reports.

Step 8: Integrations

  • Integrate communication tools: WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack.
  • Connect accounting apps: QuickBooks, Xero.
  • Link marketing platforms: MailChimp, Google Ads.
  • Cloud storage integration: Google Drive, Dropbox.
  • Install integrations via Settings > Applications.
