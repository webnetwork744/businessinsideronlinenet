About Johan Söderqvist

Johan Söderqvist is a world-renowned Swedish film composer known for his hauntingly beautiful and emotionally powerful soundtracks. He has composed music for acclaimed films such as Let the Right One In, Kon-Tiki, and Brothers, as well as the iconic Battlefield 1 soundtrack. On this channel, you can explore Johan Söderqvist’s film music, orchestral compositions, and official releases.
