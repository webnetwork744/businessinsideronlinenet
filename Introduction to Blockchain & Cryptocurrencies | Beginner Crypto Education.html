What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers. It ensures transparency, security, and immutability, making it the backbone of cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum.

What are Cryptocurrencies?

Cryptocurrencies are digital currencies that operate on blockchain technology. They enable peer-to-peer transactions without the need for banks or intermediaries. Popular examples include Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Ripple (XRP).
