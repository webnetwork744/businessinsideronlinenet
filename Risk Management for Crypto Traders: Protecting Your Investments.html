Risk management is the foundation of successful cryptocurrency trading. Learn how to safeguard your portfolio from unexpected market volatility with practical strategies.

Start Managing Risk

1. Using Stop-Loss Orders

Cutting Losses Early

Stop-loss orders automatically sell your crypto when the price falls below a set level, preventing large losses. Every crypto trader should use stop-loss as part of their risk management plan.

2. Position Sizing

Never Risk Too Much

Only allocate a small percentage of your portfolio to a single trade. Many experts suggest risking no more than 1-2% per trade to reduce the chance of major losses.

4. Trading Psychology

Controlling Emotions

Fear and greed can ruin trading decisions. Discipline, patience, and sticking to your trading plan are essential parts of crypto risk management.

Pro Tip: Always set both a stop-loss and a take-profit target for every trade. This way, you control risk and lock in profits automatically.
