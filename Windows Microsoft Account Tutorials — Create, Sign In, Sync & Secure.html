Quick start — Top actions people search for

1. How to create a Microsoft account

Search intent: users looking for a simple sign-up walkthrough ("create Microsoft account step by step").

  1. Open a browser and go to https://signup.live.com.
  2. Click Create account, enter an email (or choose a new @outlook.com address).
  3. Choose a strong password, enter your name and birthday.
  4. Verify your identity with the code sent to your email or phone.

Suggested heading for SEO: How to create a Microsoft account in 2025 — step-by-step

2. Add your Microsoft account to Windows

Search intent: users asking "how do I sign in to Windows with a Microsoft account" or "add Microsoft account to Windows 11".

  1. Open Start > Settings > Accounts.
  2. Choose Sign in with a Microsoft account instead.
  3. Enter your Microsoft email and password, complete verification.
  4. After sign-in, your settings, theme, and Edge bookmarks can sync across devices.

On-page keyword example: add Microsoft account Windows 11.

3. Switch from a local account to a Microsoft account

Search intent: "switch local to Microsoft account Windows" — users who started with a local login.

  1. Create or sign in to a Microsoft account (see step 1).
  2. Settings → Accounts → Your info → Sign in with a Microsoft account instead.
  3. Follow prompts. Your files remain on the PC; settings begin syncing.

4. Reset a forgotten Microsoft account password

Search intent: "forgot Microsoft account password" or "reset Microsoft password".

  1. Open https://account.live.com/password/reset.
  2. Choose I forgot my password, enter your email.
  3. Verify with recovery email/phone, then set a new password.

5. Secure your Microsoft account (Two-step verification)

Search intent: "enable two-step verification Microsoft" or "Microsoft Authenticator setup".

  1. Visit account.microsoft.com/security.
  2. Select Two-step verification and follow prompts.
  3. Install Microsoft Authenticator on your phone for easier verification.

6. OneDrive setup and use

Search intent: "OneDrive setup Windows 11" or "sync files to OneDrive".

  1. Open OneDrive app on Windows and sign in with your Microsoft account.
  2. Choose folders to sync and set backup options for Desktop, Documents, and Pictures.

7. Remove a Microsoft account from a PC

Search intent: "remove Microsoft account Windows" — users who want to unlink accounts.

  1. Settings → Accounts → Email & accounts.
  2. Select the account and click Remove. If it's your main login, create a local account first.

8. Link your Microsoft account to Xbox, Office & other services

Search intent: "Microsoft account Xbox link" or "use Microsoft account for Office subscription".

  • Sign in to the service with the same Microsoft email to link purchases, subscriptions, or game progress.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use a Gmail address as a Microsoft account?
Yes — you can use any email address to register and use it as your Microsoft sign-in email.
Will switching to a Microsoft account delete my files?
No. Switching only changes your sign-in method; your local files stay on the PC.
Is two-step verification necessary?
It is strongly recommended for account security, especially if you use OneDrive, Office, or Xbox.
